Game Idea:
Top down dungeon crawler where you go into a cave with different levels, to get a dumb prize at the end

Game Rules:
Walls and obstacles the player cannot pass through, enemies that will deal damage (but also die when touched), changing room designs whenever you exit through a door, a prize that you can grab when you touch it
